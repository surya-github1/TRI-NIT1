Home

Welcome to Jai Kisan, a web application dedicated to helping Indian farmers in their efforts to produce a bountiful harvest and improve their lives. Our mission is to provide information and resources to help farmers succeed in their endeavors. Whether you are a seasoned farmer or just starting out, Jai Kisan is here to help.

Many people want to do something or the other for the farmers but couldn't because they dont know what to do. Many people want to do farming but don't know how to do. This platform gathers such enthusiasts where they get to know the answers to many questions about farming which they dont know about us.

My Garden

Jai Kisan not only provides real-time information on your garden, but also sends notifications to water your plants based on the type of plants you have added to your garden. This innovative feature allows gardening enthusiasts to ensure that their plants receive the appropriate amount of water and can flourish, without having to constantly monitor their garden themselves

Enthus ki Baat

At Jai Kisan, we also provide a platform for Farmers,Enthusiasts and Experts to share their thoughts and experiences. "Kisan Ki Baat" is a forum where farmers can connect with each other and share their experiences, challenges, and successes in their farming operations. Our aim is to create a supportive community where farmers can learn from each other and work together to achieve success.
